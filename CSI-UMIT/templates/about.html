About Us

COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA
CSI-UMIT

  • Formed in the year 1965, the Computer Society of India is the first and the largest body of computer professionals in India.Today, CSI has 72 chapters all over India, 511 student branches, and more than 100,000 members.


  • The CSI UMIT chapter was formed in 1998 and it has been instrumental in providing students with wholesome technical experience. The past 17 years stand witness to numerous students of the institute benefit from this association.


  • Every year we conduct fests, workshops, industrial visits and seminars to keep the students updated about the ever-evolving technology. We also publish a yearly technical magazine called ‘Paradigm’. CSI UMIT has been an active part of TechNext India an annual academic national conference and awards for academia, organized by CSI Mumbai chapter and hosted by IIT Bombay.

  • In the last academic year, we had organized a workshops on Cyber Security and R Programming.

  • The major spectacle organized by the council is the annual inter-collegiate technical fest ‘Abhivyakti’. Abhivyakti stands for ‘expression’, thus giving the students a platform of opportunities to express their talent in both technical as well as cultural aspects. They get to widen their horizons by participating in technical as well as non-technical events and compete against their fellow college-mates along with students from various other engineering institutes in this city.


  • The theme for ABHIVYAKTI 2019 was “AGNIFROST“ which translates to the amalgamation of fire and ice. The theme is inspired by the legendary Game Of Thrones series.


  • Apart from this, we organized an industrial visit to Chennai-Mahabalipuram-Pondicherry-Auroville where we visited the at Integra Software Services Pvt. Ltd
